Resource hogs · GE Lua profiler idle
Samples BeamNG's game-engine Lua VM for ~2 s and ranks the functions that ate the most time - the closest "what's eating the frame" breakdown the game exposes to a UI. It profiles only the GE Lua thread (not C++ render/physics or the vehicle VM), adds a little overhead while sampling, then switches itself back off.

–
Click or drag the graph to scrub the cursor; mouse-wheel zooms around the pointer and the strip below pans (double-click fits the whole take). Lanes gives every channel its own scaled track, data-logger style. While Drive dashboard is on, recorded values are fed back into the panel - open the Dashboard tab and the real gauges replay the take; live telemetry is ignored until you switch it off. Actuate vehicle replays the recorded driver inputs (throttle, brake, clutch, steering, handbrake, gear) into the real car while playing - open-loop, so start from the same spot with the same vehicle for a faithful re-run; physics drift accumulates over long takes. It overrides your controller while playing; pause or stop and the controls are zeroed and handed straight back (the car keeps rolling - brake yourself).

Pre-Runner Suspension Travel

*Travel is read from each wheel's vertical contact load, though BeamNG exposes no direct suspension-travel channel, so resting load is learned as the reference: a planted wheel sits mid-track, a hard hit drives it up, a drooping or airborne wheel drops to AIR, which may also lead to related statistics working badly to not at all - depending on situation. The chip under each corner is the live ground material that tyre is on (dirt, mud, rock, gravel, sand, asphalt…), read from the wheel's contact ground-model.
Pre-Runner suspension compression graphresting load + shared scale
Each corner's live compression as a multiple of its resting load. The dashed line is static ride (1.0×); spikes above are bumps & landings - the big hits a pre-runner soaks up - and dips toward zero are the wheel extending into droop or going airborne.
Per-corner load [GP]kN · shared scale
Vertical contact load per wheel over time. Watch how fast spikes settle after bumps & landings! -> smooth decay = well-damped, lingering oscillation = under-damped.

Theme
Pick a preset or roll your own. Themes re-skin the whole panel, gauges included.
Custom CSS on
Saved locally like every other setting and carried inside profiles - skim the CSS of profiles other people share before loading them. A broken rule can't damage anything: toggle off to recover instantly.
Remote Access off
Off.
Use this panel on your phone: toggle on, open avcp.malo-interactive.net there and enter the code. Whoever enters the code can control your game, so treat it like a remote-desktop code. Off by default, never remembered across sessions, and it stops the moment you toggle off or close this tab. Both connections are encrypted (TLS) and the devices never see each other's IP - only the relay is contacted, and it stores nothing. Leave Relay server empty for the official relay, or point it at your own - the full server plus a self-hosting guide ships in the repo's server/ folder.

WARNING: WSS is basically an remote-control-exploit [RCE] when misused.
Personalization · Background & Glass
Glassmorphism off
Drop in your own image - a livery shot, a map, a wallpaper - and the panel wears it behind frosted-glass cards. Your image is stored locally in this browser's IndexedDB (sandboxed, never written to BeamNG game files) and is never uploaded anywhere. Glass uses backdrop blur; if the game feels heavy, lower the glass blur or switch glass off.

The intro plays once per browser/CEF profile, on the very first launch. If it ever re-triggers (e.g. after clearing the cache) and you'd rather never see it again, switch it off here - the setting is permanent until reset.

Interface & layout
Card layout
The layout editor works across every tab: drag cards (or use the ◀ ▶ arrows) to rearrange, hit 👁 to hide cards you never use, then Done. A saved layout survives updates only while a tab's card set is unchanged - otherwise that tab safely resets itself instead of scrambling.
Driving alerts
km/h
The shift light flashes the screen edges red when the engine passes the redline configured under Gauges; the overspeed warning holds an amber border above the threshold. Purely visual - no sounds, nothing is sent to the game.
Profiles
A profile snapshots the whole setup - theme, units, gauges, background & glass, card layout, alerts, UI scale, startup tab. Loading one applies it and reloads the panel. Exports are plain JSON you can share; an uploaded background image itself stays local (preset and online backgrounds travel fine).
Online gallery optional · CDN
Curated backgrounds and profiles from the Malo Interactive CDN. Nothing is fetched until you press Browse; applied backgrounds display straight from the CDN. Needs internet access. Want yours added? Submit it in our Discord (Credits tab) for review.
Storage & reset

Settings are saved in this browser's localStorage (key prefix avcp.); custom background images and Data Lab recordings live in this browser's IndexedDB. All of it is browser-sandboxed and never writes to BeamNG game files - clearing your browser/CEF cache resets everything. "Reset all panel settings" leaves recordings alone; delete those separately.
